California's Hidden Wine Country

LODI

  • Magical wines

    Grapes are Lodi’s history - the region was desolate until grapes brought forth their bounty. They thrive in this perfect temperate climate and loamy soil, which earned the title Lodi Appellation and the "Zinfandel capital of the world". Today there are more than 85 wineries in the region.

  • Heavenly cycling

    Cyclists love the scenic back roads of this part of the Central Valley, pausing at the many tasting room “rest stops” along the way.

  • Iconic natural beauty

    Nearby on the central California coast along the meandering byways of the California Delta, Yogi Bear's Jellystone Park offers something for everyone! This is Delta camping at its best, with Deluxe Cabins and everything you need for a great camping vacation.
